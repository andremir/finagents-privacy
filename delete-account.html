FinAgents - Delete Your Account

Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your FinAgents account and all associated data at any time. We respect your right to control your personal information.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email to privacy@finagents.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your FinAgents account
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and account information
  • All linked financial account connections
  • Stored account balances and transaction history
  • Investment holdings data
  • Any manually added accounts or custom data
  • App preferences and settings
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account and re-link your financial institutions if you wish to use FinAgents again.

Data Retained After Deletion

For legal and regulatory compliance, we may retain:

  • Records of your deletion request (for audit purposes)
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you

Any retained data will be kept for no longer than required by applicable law.

Third-Party Services

Deleting your FinAgents account will disconnect all linked accounts. However, you may also want to revoke access directly with:

  • Plaid: Visit my.plaid.com to manage connected apps
  • SnapTrade: Contact your brokerage to revoke third-party access
