FinAgents Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

FinAgents ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you connect financial accounts through our app, we collect account identifiers and balance information to display your financial overview.
  • Financial Data: We access transaction history, account balances, and investment holdings through secure third-party services (Plaid, SnapTrade) that you authorize.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device type and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your aggregated financial information
  • Provide account balance and holdings tracking
  • Generate financial summaries and analytics
  • Improve and optimize our application

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. Financial account connections are handled through secure, industry-standard services (Plaid and SnapTrade) that use bank-level encryption.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services to connect to your financial institutions:

  • Plaid: For connecting bank accounts and retrieving financial data. See Plaid's Privacy Policy.
  • SnapTrade: For connecting brokerage accounts. See SnapTrade's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect linked financial accounts at any time

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
